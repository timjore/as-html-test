Leaders, Technology, Tactics, and Trust

Why trust is the single most important aspect of high-performance teams

To the degree that the leader does not or cannot understand the tools, tactics, and methods that enable the team to achieve the objective, they absolutely must rely on and trust the team. Thus, trust is the single most crucial aspect of a high performance team, because in the increasingly complex and rapidly changing world of today, no leader can fully understand all the component pieces adequately in order to issue unilateral mandates. Milestones along the way to successful outcomes must be set, and achieved collaboratively.
